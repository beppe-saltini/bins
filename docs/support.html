Bins — Support

Bins shows your next Central Bedfordshire household bin collection (refuse or recycling) and supports a home-screen widget.

Getting started

  1. Open the app and enter your Central Bedfordshire postcode.
  2. Select your address from the list.
  3. Add the Bins widget from the home-screen widget gallery if you wish.

Not affiliated with the council

This app is independent. For official waste services, missed collections, or tidy-tip bookings, use Central Bedfordshire Council.
