Bins helps Central Bedfordshire residents see their next household bin collection at a glance.

• Enter your postcode and pick your address
• See whether the next collection is refuse (black bin) or recycling (green bin)
• Home-screen widget with the same information
• Optional app icon that switches between green and black to match the next collection

Data is stored on your device. The app reads collection dates from the council’s public website when you are online.

Not affiliated with Central Bedfordshire Council. For official services, use the council website.
